Therapeutic Goods Administrations

Dosage

Adults:
Usually 20mg a day.
Dose may vary from 10mg to 40mg a day depending on what condition you are being treated for and how severe it is.

Children (1 year or older):
10mg a day for children 10 to 20kg. This dose may be increased to 20mg if required.
For children more than 20kg, the dose is 20mg a day. This dose may be increased to 40mg if required.

Cautions

Must tell your doctor if you have:

  • Allergies to any other medicines, foods, dyes or preservatives
  • Any problems with your liver
  • Any other medical conditions
  • Been diagnosed with osteoporosis

Do not take ACIMAX if:

  • Pregnant or breastfeeding unless your doctor says so.
  • You are taking the medicine cilostazol
